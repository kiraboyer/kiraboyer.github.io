CADREzone

CADRE Student Organization, CADREzone, Arduino, VR, projection mapping, installation. 2018.

A re-imagining of the classic Twilight Zone series, the San Jose State CADRE Student Organization created a Halloween showcase as a homage to the classic series and as an exhibition of the club’s interdisciplinary skills. I was the organization’s President and created the Arduino-powered fortune teller.
