Plastic Present


Kira Boyer, Rajeev Bhayro, Akemi Sato, Plastic Present. video, projection, steel nails, 3D animation, recycled plastic. 2019.

Kira Boyer, Rajeev Bhayro, and Akemi Sato live in the San Francisco Bay Area. Living on the California coast, they have a special connection to the plastic pollution crisis of our oceans. Pollution is an ever-present, conspicuous, problem that is causing great concern in their lives and the lives of many who live on the coastlines.

In Plastic Present, these artists recognize the plastic pollution epidemic as a form that many will recognize; a pet-store bagged fish. In these plastic confinements, most fish will die before being sold, only knowing their plastic confinements; their tiny oceans. A projected cycle of life and death begins within these plastic confinements. Much like the deadly cycle of single-use plastic bags as they suffocate our oceans.
